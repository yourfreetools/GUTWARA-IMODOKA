How Icyemezo Drive Works

Learn how to get started with our platform and make the most of your driving education experience

Create Your Account

Start by creating your personal account on Icyemezo Drive. This will give you access to all our courses and practice tests.

  • Quick and easy registration process

Make Payment

Activate your account by making a payment of 1,500 FRW via mobile money to our number.

Receive Your Password

Access Learning Materials
