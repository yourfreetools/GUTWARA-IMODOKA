Payment Assistance

Having trouble with payment? We're here to help you resolve any issues quickly

Common Payment Issues

If you're experiencing any of the following issues, please contact our admin team for assistance:

  • Payment not reflecting in your account
  • Unable to complete mobile money transaction
  • Wrong amount deducted
  • Payment confirmation not received
  • Account not activated after payment
  • Any other payment-related problems

What Information to Provide

When contacting us about payment issues, please provide the following information to help us resolve your problem quickly:

  • Your full name
  • Phone number used for registration
  • Amount paid
  • Date and time of payment
  • Mobile money transaction ID (if available)
  • Description of the issue you're experiencing

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to activate my account after payment?

Accounts are typically activated within 24 hours of payment confirmation. If it's been longer than this, please contact us with your payment details.

What if I sent the wrong amount?

Contact us immediately with details of the transaction. We'll help resolve the issue by either applying the correct amount or arranging a refund if necessary.

I didn't receive a transaction confirmation. What should I do?

First, check with your mobile money provider to confirm the transaction went through. If it did but you still haven't received confirmation from us, contact us with your payment details.

Can I get a refund if I change my mind?

Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us to discuss your situation, and we'll do our best to find a satisfactory solution.
